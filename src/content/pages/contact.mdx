# Contact

This page demonstrates a **single-locale page** — it exists only in English.

## What Happens

When a page has no translation:

- Language switcher shows other locales as unavailable
- No `hreflang` tags generated for missing locales
- Page only accessible at its primary URL

## When to Use

Single-locale pages work for:

- Region-specific content
- Legal pages that differ by country
- Temporary content not worth translating

Notice the language switcher above — "Русский" is unavailable.
